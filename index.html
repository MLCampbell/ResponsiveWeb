Jinx

The Loose Cannon

Who is Jinx?

A manic and impulsive criminal from Zaun, Jinx lives to wreak havoc without care for the consequences. With an arsenal of deadly weapons, she unleashes the loudest blasts and brightest explosions to leave a trail of mayhem and panic in her wake. Jinx despises boredom, and gleefully brings her own chaotic brand of pandemonium wherever she goes.

Role

Jinx excels in the role of marksman also known as attack damage carry (adc). Jinx is a high-damge attack speed marksman that relies on sustained fights for the highest damage output. Jinx operates best out of the bottom lane (bot) and should always be paired with a strong support, especially in the early game.

Equipment

Jinx relies heavily on attack damage to scale her attack steroid pow-pow. Items with the AS stat should be prioritised, including Beserker's greaves, Statikk Shiv and Runaan's Hurricane.
